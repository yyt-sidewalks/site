About this page

The data on this page is is drawn directly from the City of St. John's site at Snow Removal - https://map.stjohns.ca/snow/

How to use this page

You can view all daily snow removal plans published by the City of St. John's, or view a specific day. The plans exist only from Feb 1, 2020 onwards. They're saved to our website on a daily basis as they're made available. Note this these plans are only for snow removal, not snow clearing off of streets or sidewalks.
