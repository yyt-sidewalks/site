Open Data Files

  • Sidewalk Priorities - Streets with Single Side Cleared (manual plotting of data from City of St. John's map)
    » singleside.json
  • Sidewalk Priorities - Priority 1 Streets (manual plotting of data from City of St. John's map)

  • Sidewalk Priorities - Priority 2 Streets (manual plotting of data from City of St. John's map)

  • Sidewalk Priorities - Priority 3 Streets (manual plotting of data from City of St. John's map)

  • Sidewalk Priorities - School Zones (manual plotting of data from City of St. John's map)
    » school_zones.json
  • Hospitals & Medical Clinics (walk-in, etc.)
    » hospitals.json
  • Schools, University & College
